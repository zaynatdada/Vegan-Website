About Us

We are an independant group of vegan food enthusiasts. With a passion for great food and ethical eating; our mission is to bring awareness to the many incredible vegan resturants that London has to offer! Whether you are looking for a decadent breakfast, a bottomless brunch with the girls or the perfect date night spot, we have created a great list to choose from.
